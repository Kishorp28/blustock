IPO Investment Guide

Comprehensive guide to help you understand and invest in IPOs effectively.

How to Subscribe to an IPO?
  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO button.
  • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
  • Step 4: Your subscription will be completed once you make the payment or give permission.
Should I buy an IPO first day?
Depends on the company's fundamentals, market demand and valuation. Review carefully before investing.
How do you know if an IPO is good?
Check DRHP, financials, peer comparison, GMP trends, and market expert opinions.
How to check IPO start date?
IPO start dates are published on NSE, BSE websites and in DRHP documents.
What is issue size?
Issue size is the total value of shares offered in the IPO to the public, expressed in Crores.
How many shares in a lot?
Lot size is predefined by the company — typically ranging from 10 to 500 shares per lot.
How is the lot size calculated?
Based on issue price band and market regulations, ensuring affordable investment size for retail investors.
Who decides the IPO price band?
Company promoters in consultation with merchant bankers decide the price band.
What is IPO GMP?
GMP (Grey Market Premium) is the unofficial price at which IPO shares trade before official listing.
How many lots should I apply for IPO?
Based on your risk appetite, GMP trends, and company valuation. Most retail investors apply 1–3 lots.
