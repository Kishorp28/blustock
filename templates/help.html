Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new IPO to the system?
Navigate to "Manage IPO" from the sidebar, click "Register IPO" button, fill in the required details including company information, price band, issue size, and dates, then save the IPO.
How can I manage user permissions?
Go to "Accounts" section, select a user, and modify their role (Admin, Staff, or User) to control their access levels and permissions within the system.
What are the different IPO statuses?
IPOs can have three statuses: "Upcoming" (not yet open), "Ongoing" (currently accepting applications), and "Listed" (successfully listed on exchange).
How do I generate API keys?
Visit the "API Manager" section, click "Generate New API Key", provide a name for the key, and it will be created with the specified permissions and rate limits.
Can I export IPO data?
Yes, you can export IPO data in various formats. Use the export buttons in the "Manage IPO" section to download data as CSV, Excel, or PDF formats.
